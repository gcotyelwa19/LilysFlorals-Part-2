About Lily’s Florals

Founded with love and rooted in nature, Lily’s Florals is a boutique floral studio offering handcrafted arrangements, seasonal specials, and a cozy café corner. Our story began in Gqeberha, inspired by the elegance of lilies and the warmth of community.
